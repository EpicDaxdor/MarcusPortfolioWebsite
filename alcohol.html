NAME OF ARTWORK: Painting in First Person

DATE OF CREATION: 20/06/2022

DESCRIPTION: This was the final I made to help me achieve a higher qualification (SQA). I did this with acrylice paint in a very warm palette. Using warm, harmonious colours made the painting have a major visual impact, almost looking like a dream. I like this piece because I experimented with an artstyle that I enjoyed. I used a dabbing motion with an old brush and alot of paint to create this "spikey" look. I also like that with this style you can see the forms of the objects so you can understand what's there but theyre slightly blurred.


NAME OF ARTWORK: Gorou Drawing

DATE OF CREATION: 27 / 02 / 2022

MEDIA/STYLE: Line Art

DESCRIPTION:This drawing is of a character from a video game called 'Genshin Impact'. The game is about twins that travel the universe exploring when they get split up and one gets dropped in a world where youre considered a complete outsider. You eventually build up your reputaion to get help from the many gods of this world known as Archons to help you guide you way back to your sibling. The character I drew is a side character that works with the player in their mission.
